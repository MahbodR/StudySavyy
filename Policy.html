PRIVACY POLICY


Privacy Policy for Study Savvy

Welcome to Study Savvy, accessible from studysavvy.me. Your privacy is critically important to us, and this document outlines the types of information we collect and record, as well as how we use it.

Log Files

Study Savvy follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. This information is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Study Savvy uses ‘cookies’ to store information, including visitors’ preferences and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the user experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors’ browser type and/or other information.

Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to studysavvy.me and other sites on the internet. However, visitors may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy.

Advertising Partners

Our advertising partners, which include Google AdSense, may use cookies and web beacons on our site. Their respective privacy policies govern the use of such cookies.

Google Analytics & Search Console

We use Google Analytics and Google Search Console to measure traffic and manage our site’s presence in Google Search results. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by visiting the Google Analytics opt-out page.

Email List Sign-Up

We offer an optional email list sign-up. By providing your email address, you agree to receive occasional newsletters and updates from Study Savvy. We use Mailchimp for our marketing platform, and your information will be transferred to Mailchimp for processing. We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties.

Children’s Information

We do not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that your child provided this kind of information on our website, please contact us immediately.

GitHub Pages Hosting

Study Savvy is hosted on GitHub Pages, a static site hosting service provided by GitHub, Inc. GitHub may collect your IP address and browser information for security purposes, which is processed in accordance with their privacy practices. Consent By using Study Savvy, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

Updates

We reserve the right to update, amend, or make any changes to our privacy policy, and those changes will be posted here.
